What We Offer

Every Kind of Uganda

🦍
Gorilla Trekking
Guided habituation and trekking experiences with Uganda's famous mountain gorillas in Bwindi and Mgahinga.
🐘
Game Drives
Sunrise and sunset safaris across Uganda's national parks to spot the Big Five and rare bird species.
🌊
Boat Cruises
Float past hippos and crocodiles on the Kazinga Channel and experience Murchison Falls from the water.
🏞️
Hiking & Trekking
Summit the Rwenzori Mountains, hike active volcano Mount Nyiragongo, and explore crater lakes.
🎭
Cultural Immersion
Visit Batwa pygmy communities, Buganda Kingdom palaces, and traditional village homestays.
🐦
Bird Watching
Uganda has over 1,060 bird species — guided birding tours for beginners and serious listers alike.
🚣
White Water Rafting
Grade 5 rapids on the mighty River Nile at Jinja — the adventure capital of East Africa.
🌙
Luxury Lodges
Wake up to wildlife views at curated eco-lodges and luxury tented camps across Uganda.
